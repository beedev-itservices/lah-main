Terms & Conditions

As of April 2nd, 2019

In an effort to keep the prices the same as well as ensure sessions are able to be properly kept, the following Terms and Conditions have been setup.

Please understand that when you book an appointment you are blocking off that time and I do have other clients that are looking to book and cancellations make it difficult to schedule.
